FRC Advanced Scouting Touchstone Privacy Policy

By using FAST, your team's scouted data is automatically uploaded and processed by FAST's servers to sync it to all of your team members' devices. Your scouted data is never made publicly available or shared with other teams without your consent. To further improve and better this app, FAST collects general usage data on how users are using the app and similarly collects crash and error reports. Please note that analytical reports will include your user id so that the developers can contact you if they need to follow up on an issue.
